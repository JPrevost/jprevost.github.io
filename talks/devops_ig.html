What is it?

  • An internal Technology group to help us DevOps.
  • Focus on both tooling (technology) and culture (people).
  • Focus on practical solutions to our existing needs, not theory.

Who is it?

An open group for all MIT Libraries Technology staff interested in furthering our real world ability to embrace DevOps culture.

  • Practical limit to participation. Let's talk.

What's next?

Scheduled interest group meetings with all interested individuals involved.
